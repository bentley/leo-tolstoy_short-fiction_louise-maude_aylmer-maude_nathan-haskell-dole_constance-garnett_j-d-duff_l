Colophon

Short Fiction
was compiled from short stories and novellas published between 1852 and 1912 by
Leo Tolstoy.
They were translated from Russian between 1888 and 1916 by
Louise Maude, Aylmer Maude and Nathan Haskell Dole.

This ebook was produced for the
Standard Ebooks project
by
Weijia Cheng,
and is based on transcriptions produced between 2012 and 2020 by
Weijia Cheng
for the
Standard Ebooks project
and
Sankar Viswanathan, Odessa Paige Turner and The Online Distributed Proofreading Team
for
Project Gutenberg (A Russian Proprietor)
and on digital scans available at the
Internet Archive (The Cossacks and Other Tales of the Caucasus)
and the
HathiTrust Digital Library (A Russian Proprietor and Sevastopol).

Religious Procession in Kursk Governorate,
a painting completed in 1883 by
Ilya Repin.
